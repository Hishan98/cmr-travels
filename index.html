Who we are

✻

Providing exceptional bus travel arrangements is the vision that’s followed at C.M.S travels so one can make the right travel arrangements for a great holiday. World largest online bus ticketing platform has driven the country’s bus booking journey over the past 15+ years through thousands of bus operators and routes.

Customer Satisfaction

✻

Bus-On-Time

customers will receive a 25% refund on their bus ticket costs in case the bus that they need to travel in is delayed by 30 minutes.

No Bus Cancellation

bus operator cancels the bus without making any alternate arrangements, the customer will receive a 150% refund on their bus ticket costs.

Alternate Assurance

If a bus breaks down during the journey, the customers will be eligible for a 300% refund on their bus tickets.
